        ## Teaching Philosophy

        My teaching is grounded in the observation that students from diverse backgrounds share a capacity for intellectual work when given the right conditions: clarity, support, and active engagement. I emphasize hands-on learning, analytical thinking, and the integration of authentic research datasets.

        ## Pedagogical Approach

        - **Active Learning**: I use the Flipped Classroom model and Think-Pair-Share exercises to move beyond passive lecturing.
        - **Real-world Context**: I anchor technical concepts (e.g., database joins, causal graphs) in familiar domains like healthcare and climate science.
        - **Frequent Assessment**: Low-stakes quizzes and interactive polls allow me to adjust pace and focus in real-time.
        - **Inclusive Instruction**: I structure my classroom so that no assumptions are made about prior preparation, and I prioritize support for underrepresented students through extended office hours.
